About me

I am a Data Engineer with 4 years of experience building data infrastructure that teams can depend on. My work spans cloud-native pipeline architecture, Lakehouse platforms, real-time streaming systems, and the data quality frameworks that keep everything reliable.

Growing up, I was drawn to mathematics and the satisfaction of working through a problem methodically. That instinct carried into my career. I find data engineering genuinely interesting because the problems are rarely straightforward -- they require thinking carefully about systems, tradeoffs, and what happens at scale.

I am naturally curious and inquisitive, the kind of person who reads about how a distributed system fails before asking how it works. That curiosity, paired with a Lean Six Sigma mindset around process and reliability, shapes how I approach every project I take on.
